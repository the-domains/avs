About Us

Applied Visual Solutions, Inc. (AVS) specializes in real-time data visualization technology to enhance utility asset management and operational performance for the utilities, manufacturing, and process industries. Our solutions provide you the following capabilities:

  • Gain insight on plant process operation with visual trending and HMI displays
  • Monitor plant key performance indicators in real-time to facilitate analysis, improve efficiency, and speed up decision-making
  • Replay plant operation in real-time for operation review and performance analysis
  • Recognize statistical patterns to detect possible faults on instrument, machine, and equipment for early alarms generation
  • Provide guidance for plant start-up, shut-down, and emergency handling
  • Access plant operation & performance remotely from anywhere in the world

In a rapidly changing energy supply and demand environment, utilities require robust control and data management tools to keep track of sprawling and aging assets. To improve operational performance and enable operator situational awareness, a broad range of data on the behavior and status of the equipment must be collected and analyzed.

Today, rapid advances in electronic sensors and monitoring devices provide owners and operators of production processes with accurate data from the SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) system. Utilities establish historian databases to collect over 30 million data points per sensor every year with the intention to improve diagnostics and aid in decision-making. However, the sheer volume of information becomes unwieldy and indecipherable without an expert system to parse, extract data trends, and generate performance reports.
